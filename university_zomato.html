        Brown Sugar is simply out of the world!!
        With just 3-4 tables like a standing arrangement outside the 2 mini shops together, it is a perfect place for getting really good snacks. Dieing to have a healthy snack and some sort of a water based mojito/shake/juice, we ended up here yesterday.
        Ordered : Strawberry Velvet + Garlic Bread. The combo was great and was priced so reasonably!!
        The Strawberry Velvet had banana chunks that lent the strawberry a different and unique flavour. The Garlic bread was baked in an oil free oven which makes it oil free. They seem to be the only one who have this unique oil free oven and have advertised this too on the main door.
        They have a great variety of shakes, juices, mocktails, smoothies and amazing snacks like momos, sandwiches etc. to choose from.
        I am definitely visiting this place more often.
